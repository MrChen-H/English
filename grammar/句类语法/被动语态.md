<font size = 6> $$被动语态$$ </font>   



##  含义 

<br>

- 被动语态是动词的一种形式,用以说明主语与` 谓语动词 `之间的关系。

        eg: He steals my heart      --->        My heart is stolen

        翻译: 他偷走了我的心          --->        我的心被他偷走了

<br>

## 各个时态的被动构成

<br>

||一般态|进行态|完成态|
|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|
|主动|do|be doing|have done|
|被动|be done|be being done|have been done|

<br>

<br>

<br>

## 一般态的被动语态

<br>

||一般现在时|一般过去时|一般将来时|
|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|
|主动|be do|was/were do|will/shall do|
|被动|be done|was/were done|will/shall be done|

<br>

        eg: Chinese is spoken in the world

<br>

<br>

## 进行态的被动语态

<br>

||现在进行|过去进行|将来进行|
|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|
|主动|be being|was/were doing|will/shall doing|
|被动|be being done|was/were being done|will/shall be being done|

<br>

        eg:This building is being built now

<br>

## 完成态的被动语态

<br>

||现在完成|过去完成|将来完成|
|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|
|主动|have/has done|had done|will/shall have done|
|被动|have/has been done|had been done|will/shall have been done|

<br>

        eg: The big bridge will have been completed

<br>

<br>

<br>

## 使用被动语态的时机

- 没有动作的执行者

        eg: The disable should be cared for.        关爱残疾人

<br>

- 动词短语的被动

        eg: A yes-man is oftne laughed at           老好人经常被嘲笑

<br>

- 情态动词的被动,情态动词+be+过去分词

        eg: This plan should be carried out as soon as possible.        这个计划应该尽快完成

<br>

- get + 过去分词 表被动

        eg: She once get hurt in love.       她曾经再爱情中受伤了.
